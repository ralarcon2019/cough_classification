What Is VOCOVID?

By providing results based on a convolutional neural network trained on a dataset labeled by licensed doctors, VOCOVID is equipped to inform users when they are exhibiting COVID-19 symptoms as opposed to another illness.

Use Cases

Healthy

Your recording indicates that you do not have COVID-19, though you may still be showing other symptoms. If in doubt, consult your local urgent care.

COVID-19

Your recording indicates symptoms of COVID-19. Please visit your local urgent care immediately for further testing and treatment.

If you continue to upload recordings daily, VOCOVID will map out the progression of your illness and even predict when you may experience new symptoms or recover.

Reporting associated symptoms such as high fever, runny nose, or body aches can further help you decide when to seek urgent medical attention.

How Do You Use VOCOVID?

      Navigate to our Login page and create an account.
      Once logged in, you'll be directed to your dashboard to record your cough and log your symptoms.
      VOCOVID will analyze your recording and alert you to any COVID-19 symptoms.

Track Your Health Over Time

VOCOVID presents your results in a simple, easy-to-understand format and updates a real-time graph so you can monitor the progression of your illness.

Disclaimer: VOCOVID is a screening tool and cannot provide an official COVID-19 diagnosis. Please consult a medical professional for an official diagnosis.
